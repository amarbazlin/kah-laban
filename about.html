About KAH-Laban

Preserving tradition, creating memories

Our Story

Our founder discovered the magic of traditional Mediterranean dairy creations—a creamy, indulgent delicacy that had been perfected over centuries. Inspired by these time-honored recipes and techniques, they saw an opportunity to reimagine this beloved treat for Sri Lankan palates. Rather than simply importing a foreign concept, we asked ourselves: How can we blend these ancient culinary traditions with the richness of Sri Lankan flavors and ingredients? This question became our mission.

What started as a passion project in a small kitchen grew into something much larger. We sourced the finest local dairy, experimented with tropical spices, and worked tirelessly to perfect our craft. Our team traveled, learning from dairy experts and understanding the science behind creating the perfect creamy texture. By combining Mediterranean dairy heritage with Sri Lankan ingredients and innovation, KAH-Laban became more than just a product—it became a bridge between cultures, honoring traditions while celebrating our home.
